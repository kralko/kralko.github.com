About

A current student in the master's program in Cinema and Media Studies at UCLA, Katy Ralko is constantly vaciliating between sheer excitement about what she's watching and reading as part of her work and broader life, and a deep desire to run away and go to culinary school.

It is the burden of a graduate student to be "forced" into writing on specific topics to fit the needs of a certain seminar and to necessarily push to the side smaller ideas. And to eat poorly. This site is meant to remedy both, in a small way, by publishing the quotidian ideas and flavors as the come up, rather than to neglect either impulse to serve the broader graduate school ideal.

To watch, eat and live. And document at least some of it.
